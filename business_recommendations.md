# Business Recommendations
## Customer Segmentation & Churn Prediction Project

---

## Executive Summary

Customers were segmented using clustering techniques and separate churn prediction models were built for each segment. This approach improves targeting efficiency and marketing ROI.

---

## Key Findings

- High-risk customers show significantly higher churn probability.
- Premium customers have high lifetime value and low churn risk.
- Month-to-month contract customers are most likely to churn.
- Contract type and tenure are the strongest predictors of churn.

---

## Segment-Based Strategy

### 1. Premium Loyal Customers
- Offer loyalty rewards
- Introduce premium bundles
- Maintain high service quality
- Upsell additional products

### 2. High-Risk Customers
- Offer retention discounts
- Encourage long-term contract upgrades
- Improve onboarding experience
- Deploy proactive customer service interventions

### 3. Moderate Customers
- Targeted cross-selling campaigns
- Personalized marketing
- Engagement improvement programs

---

## Financial Impact

Segment-based modeling allows:

- Reduction in churn rate
- Better allocation of marketing budget
- Increased customer lifetime value
- Improved retention efficiency

---

## Final Recommendation

Implement segment-specific churn prediction models in production and align marketing strategies accordingly.

This data-driven segmentation approach ensures scalable and measurable business growth.
